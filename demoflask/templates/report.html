Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new product?

To add a new product, go to the "Products" page and click the "Add Product" button. Fill in the required information and click "Save".

How do I edit an existing product?

To edit an existing product, go to the "Products" page and click the "Edit" button next to the product you want to edit. Make the necessary changes and click "Save".

How do I view my sales report?

To view your sales report, go to the "Sales" page and select the date range you want to view. Click the "View Report" button to see your sales data.
